Jarvis

Your Pinterest feed, without AI slop.

Jarvis is an iOS content curation app that connects to your existing Pinterest account and filters AI-generated pins from your home feed, boards, and search results.

What Jarvis does

  • Filters your Pinterest home feed in real time
  • Cleans up any of your boards
  • Search with human-made results prioritized
  • Save pins you choose back to your Pinterest boards
  • Track how many AI pins you blocked this week

Not a Pinterest replacement

Jarvis is a filtering layer on top of Pinterest. It uses the official Pinterest API with your permission. Your tokens are stored encrypted on our servers. Saves only happen when you tap save.
